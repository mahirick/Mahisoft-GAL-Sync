Common questions

Why does the app ask for Keychain access?

GAL Sync stores your Google OAuth tokens in the macOS Keychain — the same secure storage used by Safari and other apps for passwords. This keeps your credentials safe and off disk. Click Always Allow so the app can access the token without prompting every time.

My contacts aren't syncing. What should I check?

  • Open System Settings → Privacy & Security → Contacts and make sure GAL Sync has access.
  • Check that your Google account is listed in Preferences → Accounts and doesn't show a re-auth warning.
  • Click Sync Now from the menu bar icon to trigger an immediate sync.

I see "Re-auth needed" on my account.

Your Google OAuth token has expired and couldn't be refreshed automatically. Remove the account from Preferences → Accounts and add it again to re-authorize.

Can I sync multiple Google Workspace accounts?

Yes. Click Add Account in Preferences → Accounts for each account. Each domain gets its own contact group.

How do I uninstall?

  1. Quit GAL Sync from the menu bar (click icon → Quit).
  2. Delete Mahisoft GAL Sync.app from your Applications folder.
  3. Optionally remove app data: ~/Library/Application Support/com.mahisoft.MahisoftGALSync/
  4. Optionally remove preferences: defaults delete com.mahisoft.MahisoftGALSync
