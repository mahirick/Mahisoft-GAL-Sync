Everything you need, nothing you don't.

⚡️

Automatic sync

Set it once. The app syncs your directory every 1, 4, 12, or 24 hours — or immediately on demand.

📇

Native Apple Contacts

Contacts land in a dedicated group in your Apple Contacts — no third-party apps, no sync conflicts.

🔒

Secure by design

OAuth tokens are stored exclusively in the macOS Keychain. No data leaves your Mac.

🖼

Profile photos

Google profile photos sync automatically to each contact so faces match names.

🏢

Multiple domains

Add multiple Google Workspace accounts. Each domain gets its own contact group.

🍎

Menu bar app

Lives quietly in your menu bar. No Dock icon, no windows cluttering your desktop.

Up and running in minutes.

1

Download & install

Download the DMG, drag to Applications. Signed and notarized — no Gatekeeper warnings.

2

Sign in with Google

Click the menu bar icon → Add Account. Authorize with your Google Workspace account in your browser.

3

Contacts sync automatically

Your company directory appears in Apple Contacts. New hires and changes sync on schedule.
